## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Accessing Parakeet

1. **Open your web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. **Navigate to** the Parakeet website
3. **Landing Page** displays information about the platform

### 1.2 Creating an Account

**Option 1: Email Registration**

1. Click **"Get Started"** or navigate to `/register`
2. Enter your **Full Name**
3. Enter your **Email address**
4. Create a **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
5. Click **"Agree and Join"**
6. Your account is created and you're automatically logged in

**Option 2: Google Sign-In**

1. Click **"Sign Up with Google"**
2. Select your Google account
3. Grant permissions
4. You're automatically registered and logged in

### 1.3 Logging In

1. Navigate to `/login`
2. Enter your **Email** and **Password**
3. Click **"Log In to Parakeet"**
   - OR click **"Log In with Google"** for OAuth login
4. You're redirected to the home page

## 2. Account Management

### 2.1 Logging Out

1. Click on your **profile menu** (top right or sidebar)
2. Click **"Logout"**
3. You're logged out and redirected to the login page

### 2.2 Session Management

- Your session **persists** across browser refreshes
- You remain logged in until you explicitly log out
- If you're not logged in, you'll be redirected to login when accessing protected pages

## 3. Profile Management

### 3.1 Viewing Your Profile

1. Click **"Profile"** in the sidebar or top navigation
2. Your profile displays with:
   - Profile picture
   - Name and basic information
   - Sport, position, team, location
   - Physical stats
   - Achievements and career highlights
   - Education and experience
   - Your posts

### 3.2 Editing Your Profile

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click the **"Edit"** button (top right)
3. Update any fields:
   - **Full Name**
   - **About Me** (bio)
   - **Sport** and **Position**
   - **Team** and **Location**
   - **Physical Stats** (height, weight)
   - **Career Highlights**
   - **Key Statistics**
   - **Athletic Experience**
   - **Education**
   - **Interests & Activities**
4. Click **"Save Changes"**
5. Your profile updates immediately
6. Click **"Go Back"** to return to view mode

### 3.3 Viewing Other Users' Profiles

1. **From Search Results:**

   - Click on any athlete's name or profile card
   - Their profile opens in a new view

2. **From Posts:**

   - Click on the author's name in any post
   - Navigate to their profile

3. **Profile Information Displayed:**
   - All public profile information
   - Their posts
   - Their achievements and stats

## 4. Social Features

### 4.1 Creating a Post

1. Navigate to the **Home** page (`/home`)
2. In the center section, find the **post creation box**
3. Click on **"Start a post"**
4. A modal window opens
5. Type your post content in the text area
6. (Optional) Click **"Photo"** to upload an image
7. Click **"Post"** to publish
8. Your post appears in the feed immediately

### 4.2 Viewing Posts

- Posts display in the **center feed** on the home page
- Posts show:
  - Author name (clickable to view profile)
  - Timestamp
  - Post content
  - Image (if included)
  - Like count
  - Comment count

### 4.3 Liking a Post

1. Find a post you want to like
2. Click the **heart icon** (â¤ï¸) below the post
3. The heart turns red and the count increases
4. Click again to unlike

### 4.4 Commenting on a Post

1. Click **"Comment"** below a post
2. The comment section expands
3. Type your comment in the input field
4. Click **"Post"** to submit
5. Your comment appears immediately
6. Click **"Comment"** again to collapse the section

### 4.5 Deleting Your Comments

1. Open the comment section on a post
2. Find your comment
3. Click **"Delete"** next to your comment
4. The comment is removed immediately

## 5. Search & Discovery

### 5.1 Basic Text Search

1. Navigate to the **Search** page (`/search`)
2. Use the **search bar** at the top
3. Type an athlete's name or keyword
4. Results display in the center panel
5. Click on any result to view their profile

### 5.2 Using Filters

**Available Filters:**

- **Sport:** Basketball, Soccer, Football, Baseball, Tennis, Golf, Swimming, Track & Field, Volleyball, Other
- **Position:** Any position name (e.g., Point Guard, Striker, Quarterback)
- **Location:** City, State, or Country
- **Team:** Team name
- **Education:** School or university name
- **Experience:** Rookie, Junior, Mid, Senior, Veteran
- **Height:** Physical height
- **Weight:** Physical weight

**How to Use Filters:**

1. Navigate to the **Search** page
2. Use the **filter panel** on the left side
3. Select filters from dropdowns or enter text
4. Results update automatically as you change filters
5. Multiple filters can be applied simultaneously

### 5.3 Search History

1. Your **recent searches** appear on the right sidebar
2. Click on any previous search to repeat it
3. Search history includes:
   - Query text
   - Applied filters
   - Timestamp

### 5.4 Understanding Search Results

- Results display as **profile cards** showing:
  - Name
  - Sport and position
  - Location
  - Profile picture (if available)
- Click any card to view full profile
- Results update in real-time as filters change

## 6. AI Chatbot

### 6.1 Accessing the Chatbot

1. Navigate to the **Search** page
2. Look for the **chat icon** (ðŸ’¬) in the bottom right corner
3. Click the icon to open the chat panel

### 6.2 Using Natural Language Search

**Example Queries:**

- "Find me tall basketball players"
- "Show me swimmers from Miami"
- "Find basketball players in Miami who went to Duke"
- "I'm looking for point guards"

**How It Works:**

1. Type your query in natural language
2. The AI understands your intent
3. Extracts relevant filters
4. Executes the search
5. Displays results with an explanation

### 6.3 Chatbot Features

**Search Assistance:**

- Ask for athletes by sport, location, position, etc.
- Use natural language (no need to learn filter syntax)
- Get help understanding how filters work

**Context-Aware:**

- The chatbot remembers your conversation
- Follow-up questions work naturally
- "Find me more like that" references previous results

**Help & Guidance:**

- Ask "How do filters work?" for guidance
- Get explanations of platform features
- Learn about search capabilities

### 6.4 Chatbot Tips

**Best Practices:**

- Be specific: "Find basketball point guards in Miami" is better than "find athletes"
- Use natural language: "tall players" works, not just "height > 6'0"
- Ask follow-ups: "What about in Los Angeles?" after a Miami search

**Example Conversation:**

```
You: "Find me basketball players"
AI: "I found 25 basketball players. Would you like to filter by location?"
You: "Yes, Miami"
AI: "I found 5 basketball players in Miami. Here are the results..."
You: "Show me the point guards"
AI: "I found 2 point guards in Miami from the previous results..."
```

## 7. Troubleshooting

### 7.1 Login Issues

**Problem:** Can't log in

- **Solution:** Check email and password are correct
- **Solution:** Try "Forgot Password" if available
- **Solution:** Try Google Sign-In as alternative
- **Solution:** Clear browser cache and cookies

**Problem:** Redirected to login when already logged in

- **Solution:** Refresh the page
- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Try logging out and back in

### 7.2 Search Issues

**Problem:** No search results

- **Solution:** Try broader search terms
- **Solution:** Remove some filters
- **Solution:** Check spelling
- **Solution:** Try the AI chatbot for semantic search

**Problem:** Search is slow

- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Reduce number of filters
- **Solution:** Wait a moment and try again

### 7.3 Profile Issues

**Problem:** Can't edit profile

- **Solution:** Make sure you're viewing your own profile
- **Solution:** Look for "Edit" button in top right
- **Solution:** Refresh the page

**Problem:** Changes not saving

- **Solution:** Check all required fields are filled
- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Try saving again

### 7.4 Post Issues

**Problem:** Post not appearing

- **Solution:** Refresh the page
- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Verify post was successfully created (check for success message)

**Problem:** Can't upload image

- **Solution:** Check image file size (should be reasonable)
- **Solution:** Try a different image format (JPG, PNG)
- **Solution:** Check internet connection

### 7.5 Chatbot Issues

**Problem:** Chatbot not responding

- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Wait a moment (AI processing takes time)
- **Solution:** Try rephrasing your question
- **Solution:** Refresh the page

**Problem:** Chatbot gives wrong results

- **Solution:** Be more specific in your query
- **Solution:** Try using filters directly
- **Solution:** Ask the chatbot for clarification

### 7.6 General Issues

**Problem:** Page not loading

- **Solution:** Check internet connection
- **Solution:** Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- **Solution:** Clear browser cache
- **Solution:** Try a different browser

**Problem:** Features not working

- **Solution:** Make sure you're logged in
- **Solution:** Check browser console for errors (F12)
- **Solution:** Try logging out and back in

## 10. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: How do I change my password?

**A:** Currently, password changes must be done through Firebase Auth. Use "Forgot Password" on the login page.

### Q: Can I delete my account?

**A:** Account deletion is not currently available in the UI. Contact support for account deletion.

### Q: How do I report inappropriate content?

**A:** Use the report feature (if available) or contact support.

### Q: Can I message other users directly?

**A:** Direct messaging is a planned feature but not currently available.

### Q: How does the AI chatbot work?

**A:** The chatbot uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to understand your queries and find relevant athletes from the database using semantic search.

### Q: Is my data secure?

**A:** Yes, your data is stored securely in Firebase with proper security rules. Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth.

### Q: Can I use Parakeet on mobile?

**A:** Yes, Parakeet is responsive and works on mobile browsers, though it's optimized for desktop.

### Q: How do I upload a profile picture?

**A:** Profile picture upload is a planned feature. Currently, default avatars are used.
